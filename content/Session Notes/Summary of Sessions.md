## Summary of Sessions

### Overview:
This document provides a chronological summary of the Dungeons & Dragons campaign sessions, detailing key events, plot developments, and introductions of characters and locations. It serves as a central hub for tracking the campaign's progression.

#### Session 0 - 5/25/25

#### Session 1 - 6/6/25

- **Key Events:** Fought goblins.

#### Session 2 - 6/8/25

- **Key Events:**
  - Introduction to [[Vo'dyn Empire|Vo'dyn]] prayer and religious tenets.
  - Heading to [[Zen'tar]], a city experiencing political instability due to the king’s recent death.
  - Information about Zentar's geography (between two giant rivers, sub-capital).
  - Details on Zentar's political landscape: old duke died, prince missing, church attempting takeover, past tithe issues.
  - Mention of criminal organizations working inconsistently with guilds.
  - Introduction to faint elements of Abjuration in the universe.
  - Encounter with [[mist]] wraiths, noting their weakness to radiant damage.

#### Session 3 - 6/14/25

- **Key Events:**
  - Discussion about securing the prince and a plan involving a "paradigm" and "diadem."
  - Further details on Zentar's features: Stork Industries, Extending Life research, special ore mining.
  - Confirmation of the Duke's death and the missing prince.

#### Session 4 - 6/18/25

- **Key Events:**
  - Learning about disappearances around the destination.
  - Last known disappeared at AX42 Sanitarium block.
  - Identification of Sanitarium and Sunken Croc as locations on the outskirts.
