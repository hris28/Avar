## Vo'dyn Empire

### Overview:
The Vo'dyn Empire is a vast political entity structured into approximately 9-11 concentric "circles," each defining the characteristics of its inhabitants and regions.

### Structure:
- **Circles:** The empire is organized into 9-11 concentric circles, with each circle representing different social strata and geographical areas.
- **First Circle (Capital):** The innermost circle is the Capital, [[Kholinar]]. This is the primary residence of the high nobility, who govern the empire. The political system is heavily influenced by the high lords, who are presumed to answer to a higher authority.
- **Second Circle (Religious Sector):** Expanding outwards, the second circle is dedicated to the religious sect. Churches and priests are predominantly located here, actively seeking to convert outsiders to their faith, which adheres to the tenets of "[[Him]]."
- **Outer Circles (Social Hierarchy):** Beyond the religious sector, the circles are inhabited by lower nobility and common workers. Areas closer to the capital generally exhibit better living conditions and cleanliness, reflecting the societal values.
- 6th circle - [[dav ki]] hold. bastions. [[Zen'tar]].

### Core Values & Principles:
- **Cleanliness and Faithfulness:** These are highly esteemed values within the Vo'dyn society.
- **Foundation of Government:** The current system of government was established upon faith, which played a crucial role in rebuilding society after a catastrophic event known as the Cataclysm.

### Tithe System:
- All citizens are mandated to pay a tithe, which is assessed based on their overall contribution to society and their faithfulness.
- Individuals with extreme or specific ailments/disabilities may face restrictions in various positions, or even execution, if they are deemed unable to contribute or are unfaithful.

### Enforcement (The Inquisitors):
- The [[Inquisition]] are responsible for enforcing the tithe and suppressing heresy.
- They possess the authority to execute individuals instantly, regardless of noble status, for non-payment of tithes or engaging in hearsay. Such individuals are promptly replaced.

### Key Locations & Concepts:
- [[Zen'tar]]: A sub-capital within the empire, indicating its significance beyond Kholinar.
- [[Kholinar]]: The capital city, central to the empire's governance and nobility.
- [[Him]]: The deity or central figure of the empire's religion.
