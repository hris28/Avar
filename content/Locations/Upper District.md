## Upper District

### Overview:
The Upper District is a specific area within the city of [[Zen'tar]]. It is notable for housing the [[Investigator Station]].

### Key Locations:
- [[Investigator Station]]: A significant facility for investigative work, located within this district.

### Connections:
- Part of [[Zen'tar]].
