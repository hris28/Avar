## Zen'tar

### Overview:
Zen'tar is a regional capital and a sub-capital within the [[Vo'dyn Empire]]. It is a city of decent size, known for its expansive facilities and opportunities. Its political landscape is currently unstable due to the recent death of its beloved, but perhaps too soft, Duke.

### Geography & Accessibility:
- Located between two giant rivers, making it not easily accessible.

### Political Climate:
- Experiencing political instability following the King's death.
- The old [[Duke]], who brought the city to prominence, has also died.
- The [[Prince]] is missing (potentially enlisted).
- The church is attempting to gain more control, leading to inter-council conflicts.
- There was a past issue with tithe enforcement, which has since been sorted out.

### Key Features & Industries:
- **[[Medical Institute]]:** Known for extending life.
- **Mining Town/Hub:** Brought to prominence by the Duke, with special ore mining.
- **Engineering Wing:** Involved in weapons testing and developing new technology to balance might and intellect.
- **[[Stork Industries]]:** A notable city feature.

### Organizations & Divisions:
- **[[Penal Outriders]]/Penitent Outriders:** A penal division operating within Zen'tar, which the player characters are part of (see [[Summary of Sessions#Session 5]]).
- **Penal Hassars:** Half of these are present in Zen'tar.

### Places to Investigate:
- Medical and research institute.
- Engineering wing, for weapons testing and new tech.
- Personal libraries of the Duke.
- AX42 Sanitarium block (last known location of disappearances, see [[Summary of Sessions#Session 4]]).
- Sunken Croc (on the outskirts, see [[Summary of Sessions#Session 4]]).
