Ryo Dayana lived in the 8th circle on a farm, the [[Bandlewoods]]. One day, when exploring a cave he frequented in the middle of the forests near his farm, he found a powerful moster--a Chimera--hiding in his cave, having been running from wizards. Ryo ran into this creature at its last breath, and noticed a finger hanging out of the beast's mouth. A finger in perfectly good condition, with no blood on it. He was drawn to it, and upon picking it up, his vision went black. The last thing he hears before losing consciousness is "FINALLY, AFTER MILLENIA, I HAVE FINALLY FOUND A SUITABLE ONE."
	As he came to, he didn’t feel physically much different. Although, his head felt… clearer than usual. The finger disappeared. Although, the corpse was still there. Going back home and reporting the unusual corpse to the authorities, Ryo tried to clear his head. He must have imagined the finger and the voice. It was just a reaction to such a shocking scene. That night, he saw a shocking scene in his dreams. His farm, burning. His family, dead on the floor with wizards standing above them. And, a wand pointed to his forehead. Jolting awake, Ryo lay panting on his bed. But then, he heard the voice again. “*You didn’t imagine me by the way*”.
“*The (insert order) was hunting for that finger. You’ve committed a grave mistake by taking it.*”
Ryo: "who r u?"
(Insert name of magician from past here)
“*The finger was mine. I left it as a means of resurrection in the future, a sort of failsafe. Its inside you now.*”
Ryo: “Why the hell did I absorb it instantly?”
“*It's meant to only work for a suitable host. Anyway, you might want to run.*”
Ryo: “Why”
"*The visions you saw. That's what will happen if they find you. They can track the magical signature of the finger. You’re stuck with me now, let me help.*"
Waking up, Ryo realizes that he is now a level 2 sorcerer. He packs a few things, and leaves.
