- [[Decima]] was trained to be a scholar primarily. She was fine with that life growing up but she was alone. Most of her siblings were at least a decade older than her and her mother and father were absent with their own duties.
- Her closest friend was her older sister, [[Nona]]. She was a great creative soul and they were very close in their childhood. Decima thought they would be close for their entire life. But then at 18 her sister got married to a clergy based house, and Decima hasn't seen her for 7 years or longer. The loss of her sister made her begin to question much of what she believed to be true. Lowkey went crazy and had manic episodes thinking "what is even the point of Him". Decima began to become a heretic after this, and looked elsewhere. Over the course of a few months, she was able to use vacationing homes in the 6th circle and began diving into knowledge that she likely shouldn't have. She delved into research along the lines of "changing [[Fate]]."
- Found a book in the stores of her house. The book is later known to be a tome from the apprentice of the entity that is inhabiting [[Ryo]].
- Has used thousands of runes to open a portal. Her ritual was perfect. Dark Green eyes (?). “Every bargain has a price.” Every sigil gets wiped, turns on fire. Something wormed its way into her head.
	- Summoning point

- Contacted and made a deal with an entity from a different realm of existence that is eating away her memories. She doesn't remember the deal she made.
- Woke up with the loss of her vision and speech and this presence in her brain. 
	- Entity in Decima's head - From a different plane/realm of reality. She made a deal with it but doesn't know what the deal was.
