Thog Mossass
### Overview
**Echo Keyword**: [[ROOT]]
**Race:** Firbolg
**Class:** Druid
**Character Sheet:** [Here]()
**Aliases:** “Corvus”, “The Devil of 19B”, “The Last Druid of the Wailing Vale”, “The Pruning Hand”, “Old Fester-feathers” (derogatory), “Saint of Spores” (heretical)

**Classification:** Pagan druid. Suspected eco-terrorist. Exhibits delusions of grandeur and cult-like influence. Subject may act as vector for ideologically contagious belief systems related to penance and the web of life's ability to adapt and survive.

**Description:** Humanoid, presumed wood elf descent. Appears cloaked in patchwork of bark, twine, feathers, and moss. Face obscured by archaic plague helm of indeterminate origin. Carries a gnarled bough believed to serve as both weapon and ritual implement.

**Operational Notes:** Rare but consistent direct sightings. Frontier populations speak of a reckoner to those who "take without giving." Pagan oral tradition reveres a “virulent guardian” credited with both healing plagues and delivering them, or the “corvid counsel of nature herself” who compels adaptation and symbiosis.

### Role in Party:
Thog is one of the player characters in the campaign.
