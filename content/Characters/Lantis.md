## Lantis

### Overview:
Lantis is a junior investigator characterized by a "good heart" and a commoner background.

### Role, Relationships, & Characteristics:
- Junior investigator.
- Lead investigator of disappearences.
- Reports to [[Arthur]] concerning ongoing disappearances.
- Frequents Tavern of the [[Sunken Croc]].
- Has a goatee.
- Not highborn.
- Probably is dead (devil's bargain)
- Half-elf (has been called "mudblood")
