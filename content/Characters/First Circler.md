## First Circler

The First Circler is an individual of significant importance, specifically noted as someone [[Garth]], the deputy sergeant in arms, is keen to impress. This suggests a high-ranking position or considerable influence within the administrative or magisterial hierarchy, possibly originating from the [[Vo'dyn Empire|First Circle]] (Kholinar) where high nobility resides.

### Role & Significance:
- A person of note.
- Holds a position or influence that commands respect from figures like [[Garth]].
- Most likely referring to [[Secus]].

### Connections:

- Potentially linked to the [[Vo'dyn Empire|First Circle]] of the Vo'dyn Empire, implying high noble or administrative status.
