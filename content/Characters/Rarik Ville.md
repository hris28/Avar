## Rarik Ville

### Overview:
Rarik Ville is the Head Justice, often seen in a "brown robe," which might signify his role or affiliation. There's an open question about whether he is perceived as "scary."

### Role:
- Head Justice, responsible for maintaining order and ensuring compliance.
- Likely operates within [[Zen'tar]], given his role as a justice.
- Inquisitor (allegedly)
- Allegedly "traveling through"
- The inquisitor seems to understand the politics of the city extremely well for someone that was apparently traveling through.
