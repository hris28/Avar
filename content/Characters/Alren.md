[[Mithe]]'s last correspondence with Alren Davarre:
> My dear child,
> 
> You were not meant to come into my care, yet when you did, the choice was already made. I told myself it was duty, but time has a way of softening duty into something harder to name. 
> 
> I have long known the [[mask]] you wear. The laughter, the charm, the warmth you offer so freely,  they are not lies, only armor. You learned early that brightness can hide the tremor in the hand, that a smile can hold back the dark. There is no shame in that. The world needs masks to remember what faces once looked like.
> 
> There are loyalties I cannot speak of, and one among them will one day call to you. When it does, follow it. Do not flinch from what you find. Beneath the mask you have made is the same [[truth]] that binds us all: power, pain, and the cost of keeping both.
> 
> You were never a mistake, only a reminder of *{smudged}* what could be. 
> 
> Should you ever forget your purpose, look to the still water. It remembers what even the stars have lost. The reflection endures.
> ~Alren Davarre
