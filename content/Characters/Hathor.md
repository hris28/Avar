## Hathor

### Overview:
Hathor holds the title of Militant Adjudicant, suggesting a role combining military authority with judicial or administrative functions. They are a member of a cadet branch of the [[House Mournen]], a noble family whose original line produced a Duke.

### Affiliations:
- Cadet branch of the [[House Mournen]].
- Associated with [[House Bluefist]], which is also a cadet branch of the House of Mournen.
