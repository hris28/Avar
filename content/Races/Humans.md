## Humans

### Overview:
Humans are a prominent race with complex relationships with other races and a notable societal structure.

### Relationships:
- **[[Orcs]]:** Initially hostile, but some Orc tribes have converted and now fight alongside humans.
- **[[Dwarves]]:** Maintain a pragmatic, cooperative relationship born out of necessity.
- **[[Elves]]:** Exhibit a strong distrust towards elves in general.

### Societal Structure:
- Possess the second most hierarchical society, following only the [[Dwarves]].
