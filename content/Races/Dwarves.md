## Dwarves

### Overview:
Dwarves are a race known for their highly hierarchical society.
Dense, apparently. At least according to [this preamble message](https://discord.com/channels/1371908576078987465/1371908576096026701/1374481643255103570).
Have darkvision.

- Dwarves and humans have a neutral/friendly relationship "out of necessity."
- Dwarves initially gave humans some of their settlements becase they were weak and needed garrisons (Perhaps a reference to the [[Dav Ki]] civilization?). 
- Dwarves dislike elves (aside from wood elves).
- Dwarves have the most hierarchical society, humans coming second.
### Relationships:
- **[[Humans]]:** Maintain a pragmatic relationship out of necessity, having initially provided humans with settlements.
- **[[Elves]]:** Generally dislike elves, with the exception of [[Wood Elves]].
