## Elves

### Overview:
Elves are a diverse race with varying relationships with other races and a generally less hierarchical societal structure compared to others, with the exception of the [[Aeilin (High Elves)|Aeilin (High Elves)]].
Less prone to disease.
### Relationships:
- **[[Dwarves]]:** Generally disliked by dwarves, with the exception of [[Wood Elves]].
- **[[Humans]]:** Greatly distrusted by humans.

### Societal Structure:
- Lack a significant hierarchy, except for the [[Aeilin (High Elves)]].

### Sub-races:

- [[Dark Elves]]
- [[Wood Elves]]
