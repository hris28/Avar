## Wood Elves

### Overview:
Wood Elves are a neutral faction among the Elven sub-races.

### Relationships:
- **[[Aeilin (High Elves)]] & [[Dark Elves]]:** Both High and Dark Elves are ambivalent towards Wood Elves due to their neutrality.
- **[[Dwarves]]:** Unlike all of the other Elves, Wood Elves are liked by Dwarves.
