## Dark Elves

### Overview:
Dark Elves are a race with a significant history, notably responsible for bringing the [[Urd]] from another world.

### Relationships:
- **[[Aeilin (High Elves)]]:** Mutually hate each other.
- **[[Wood Elves]]:** Ambivalent towards them, as Wood Elves are neutral.

### Connections:

- Brought the [[Urd]] to this world.
