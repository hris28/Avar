## **The Recreance** 

**Date of Origin:** 427 
**Date Found:** 576 

> Observations indicate that many of the 'magically attuned' races have undergone a profound transformation. These races appear to have become more mundane, grounded in our new reality. The exact cause remains shrouded in mystery, yet it is undoubtedly linked to potent, pre-cataclysmic magic. Despite these changes, these 'race-bound' beings can still access their intrinsic abilities through the [[Ether]], though their physical manifestations are now confined. Extensive research is imperative to fully comprehend the ramifications of these transformations. 

**Author:** Unknown 
**Location:** Abandoned Labratory containing pre-cataclysm equipment.
