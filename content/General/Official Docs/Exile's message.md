No plea, no question, only vision.

A figure drifts where gravity should end, flesh drained to light, ribs bound with [[hymn]]-script that glows and fades with each syllable the Empire still sings. 
His voice arrives before his mouth moves. 

I was cast beyond the margin yet the song kept my pulse. 
I trespass on the quiet because silence stole my name. 
If the [[veil]] is shut I will walk through the hymn instead. 
Remember me when the counting fails. 
Call me [[Exile]], for even the night refused to hold me. 

The vision [[fracture]]s. Each [[shard]] shows the same face at a different age.
