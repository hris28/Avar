
**Date of Origin:** Unknown 

> The scholars of Hollowdeep whisper of a hidden realm layered beneath the [[ether]] ... a second planar domain inhabited by [[fracture]]d 'entities', severed from something...perhaps related to the Cataclysm above. These entities, twisted reflections of their surface counterparts, are less vibrant, more volatile. Yet, they exhibit a desperate sentience, as though aware of their [[exile]]. 
> 
> Some believe this inner planar was a creation of necessity, a severed [[echo]] to preserve magic after the Cataclysm. Others suspect a darker purpose. Whatever its origin, this “Second [[Veil]]” is the [[key]] to understanding the strange inconsistencies in magic’s resurgence. It is accessible only at the confluence of silence, memory, and grief.
