
> In the year of our lord 626, the world has been irrevocably altered. Four hundred and twenty-six years past, the great cataclysm reshaped existence. The revered fathers chronicle an age long gone—a time when magic flowed unhindered, when dragons and majestic beasts ruled the skies, and the mighty empire of Vo'dyn reigned supreme in its capital of [[Kholinar]]. 
> 
> Now, it lies as a stagnated corpse, a shadow of its former grandeur. It was an epoch of both heresy and unparalleled technological and magical marvels. The cataclysm came as divine retribution for our faltering faith. 
> 
> In its wake, the devout sought refuge in the vast, labyrinthine cave systems of [[Avar]], seizing the ruins of the ancient Dav Ki civilization and ensuring the survival of humankind. For the lord is our shield, our sword, and our final refuge.
