## Dwarven Report: The Rising Tide

**Date of Origin:** 622 

> The Dwarven Kingdoms, steadfast and enduring, have noted a disconcerting increase in hostilities. The [[Urd]]- those brought from another world by the [[Dark Elves]] have surfaced once again. Reports are returning of ferocious orcish warriors that have grown bolder, launching relentless raids upon our fortresses. In the shadows, the Dark Elven lords, once elusive, now wander our borders with malevolent intent, their motives inscrutable and their presence a growing threat. 
> 
> Our once impenetrable halls now echo with the sounds of battle, and our vigilance must be unwavering. The ancient tunnels and strongholds, bastions of our heritage, are under siege as never before. The resurgence of the old magics, though whispered among our scholars, remains largely unknown to us. We stand on the precipice, our hammers ready, our hearts resolute.
