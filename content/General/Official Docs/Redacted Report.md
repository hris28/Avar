## The Future
An opening to the surface world has been uncovered. Its origins remain unknown, but we now cast our gaze forward, determined to reclaim our lost glory, power, and magic. Signs of this resurgence are already evident—the ancient magics are reawakening. The All-Fathers and Mothers proclaim it as the return of the lord, while ancient guilds of arcane practitioners argue that the [[Ether]] has been restored. Our focus is steadfast on the future. 

### Redacted Report:
> To the great Highlords of Vo'dyn, 
> 
> There are others beyond our borders...their identity, motives, and potential involvement in the disappearance of the [[Aeilin (High Elves)]] remain elusive. They have rebuffed all attempts at communication. No expedition party sent to investigate has ever returned. May the lord preserve us. 

**Dated:** In the year of our lord, 616 
**Sealed by:** Highlord [[Xzal]] of [[Banmore Keep]] (Location of the Tenth Circle, on the edge of [[Avar]])
