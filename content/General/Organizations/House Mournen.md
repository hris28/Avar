## House of Mournen

### Overview:
The House of Mournen is a noble house. It has an original branch, from which a Duke originated, and a cadet branch.

### Affiliations:
- Its cadet branch is associated with [[House Bluefist]] and [[Characters/Hathor]].

### Connections:
- The former Duke of [[House Mournen]] is from the original branch.
- Mentioned in [[House Bluefist]] and [[Characters/Hathor]].
