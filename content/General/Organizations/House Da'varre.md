Sigil: Glass Prism.

A cadet house of a far more ancient line that was said to have mostly been extinguished. One of the known 9 from the original 12 houses.
[[House Da'varre]] is a line of archivists and record-keepers who dwell in the [[Gloamspire]], a tower rising from the still waters of the cavern’s central lake. Once scholars of light and reflection, they now serve as quiet stewards of history in a world that has forgotten its own past. Their halls are lit by glass lanterns and etched mirrors said to glow faintly when truth is spoken nearby. 

Though their title is minor, the Davarre hold influence through their knowledge. They record what others bury and lend their scribes to powerful patrons who prefer discretion. Count [[Alren]] Da'varre, the last of his line (and rumored to trace his lineage back to the old unknown great house that Davarre is descended from), is a careful and patient man whose loyalty lies in silence.
