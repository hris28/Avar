Section I

The Great Houses of Avar

⚑ The Original Twelve — What Is Known
The Vo'dyn Empire was founded alongside 12 Great Houses in the immediate aftermath of the Cataclysm. Records are imprecise — confirmed by DM as "9 or 11 or 12 but records are imprecise." Of these 12:

• 9 are known and survive in some form: Khol, Lanvar, Sadin, Vostin, Relkan, Velthara, Rayne, Calder, Korvath, and Branneth are among the known original houses.
• House Da'varre is not itself one of the original 12 — it is a cadet branch of a far more ancient house that was "mostly extinguished." Count Alren is rumored to trace his lineage to that lost original directly.
• 1 house was lost to time — no records, no name, no known descendants.
• 2 houses were expunged: one fell during the Great Schism (during the years of the Great Martyred Lady, when the Church asserted dominance over nobles); the second was erased for "direct opposition to the Church's mandate." Both bloodlines ended.

Note: Houses Mournen, Domitae, Maelis, Morren, Voss, and all cadet branches (Bluefist, etc.) are NOT among the original 12.
